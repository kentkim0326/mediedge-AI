The Problem

Rural clinics have X-rays.
They have no one to read them.

Across APAC, thousands of clinics own diagnostic imaging equipment — but lack the radiologists needed to interpret results. Patients suffer. Technology sits idle.

⚠️
Specialist Shortage
Korea's government relaxed MRI specialist requirements in 2026 — proof the rural healthcare crisis is worsening. 1,500+ clinics have X-ray equipment with no one qualified to read results.
🕐
2+ Hour Delays
Cloud-based radiology reads cost ~$80 per image and take over 2 hours. Emergency patients travel 1–2 hours to city hospitals — often at serious medical risk.
📡
Cloud AI Won't Reach
Existing AI radiology platforms (Lunit, Vuno, JLK) target large hospitals with stable internet. Rural and island clinics with unreliable connectivity are left behind entirely.
Our Solution

On-Device X-ray AI.
Works anywhere.

MediEdge runs entirely on a Qualcomm Dragonwing chip — delivering expert-level fracture detection with no internet, no cloud, no subscription per read.

01
Patient Arrives
Patient presents at rural health center. Standard X-ray is captured using existing equipment.
02
Image Transfer
DICOM/JPEG image is sent to MediEdge device via local Wi-Fi or USB. No internet needed.
03
⚡ AI Analysis in <3 Seconds
Qualcomm NPU processes the image on-device. Fracture location, confidence score, and urgency level are displayed instantly.
04
Clinical Decision
GP or nurse uses AI result as decision support — treat on-site or refer immediately. Patient data stays local.
🔒
100% On-Device Privacy
Patient data never leaves the clinic. Full HIPAA & PIPA compliance by design.
⚡
Qualcomm NPU Powered
INT8-optimized model runs at under 10W on Dragonwing QRB2210. Clinic-friendly power.
💰
80% Cost Reduction
$3,500 one-time device vs. $80/read ongoing cloud fees. ROI in under 6 months.
🌐
Works Completely Offline
Island clinics, mountain villages, disaster zones — MediEdge works wherever patients are.
🎯
88%+ Detection Accuracy
